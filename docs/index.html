🔒 Privacy Policy

iNOTHING App — Last updated: June 29, 2026

Summary: iNOTHING does not directly collect personal data. The app uses Google AdMob to display ads, which may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Information We Collect

iNOTHING itself does not directly collect any personal information. However, the app integrates third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) which may collect certain data to serve advertisements.

  • No personal data (like name, email, or contacts) is collected directly by us.
  • Device advertising identifiers and network data are collected by third-party ad networks.
  • No camera, microphone, or precise location data is accessed by the app.

2. Data Storage

All app data (such as your current page number) is stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server or third party.

3. Third-Party Services

iNOTHING uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for approximate location-based ads)

This data is collected and processed by Google according to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this data.

4. Permissions

iNOTHING requires internet access to display advertisements. The app does not access your contacts, storage, camera, microphone, or any other device features.

5. Children's Privacy

iNOTHING is safe for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. However, third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) may collect standard advertising data as permitted by their policies.

6. Internet Access

iNOTHING requires internet access to load and display advertisements via Google AdMob. The core app functionality works offline.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
